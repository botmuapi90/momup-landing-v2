Result: A clear target role backed by data, not guesswork
2

Prepare — "Show Up as the Obvious Choice"

The system builds your professional toolkit for your specific target role. Your resume gets analyzed and optimized for keyword alignment and ATS readability. Your LinkedIn profile gets rewritten — headline, about section, experience entries. You get interview prep with behavioral questions, company research tools, and negotiation scripts. Nothing is generic. Every asset speaks directly to the role you're pursuing.

Result: A complete professional presence built for your target role
3

Pursue — "Follow the Plan, Get Interviews"

This is where MomUp Studio becomes unlike anything else. The system automatically finds job opportunities matching your target role and delivers a daily action plan — a short list of specific moves for each job. "Tailor your resume for this listing. Send this connection request. Prepare for this phone screen." You don't plan. You don't strategize. You just follow the steps.

Result: A daily checklist that turns job searching into a manageable routine
